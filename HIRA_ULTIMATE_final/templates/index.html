Ethics & Transparency

Responsible AI in Healthcare

HIRA is built with ethical principles at its core. We believe in transparent, explainable AI that empowers—never replaces—healthcare professionals.

What AI Does

  • Flags potential findings for doctor review
  • Compares with patient history for context
  • Provides confidence scores and explanations
  • Generates clear, readable summaries
  • Helps prioritize urgent cases

What AI Does NOT Do

  • Provide medical diagnoses
  • Replace doctor's clinical judgment
  • Make treatment decisions
  • Guarantee accuracy or completeness
  • Serve as a substitute for medical consultation
Academic Project Disclaimer

HIRA Ultimate is an academic demonstration project designed for educational purposes. It is NOT intended for clinical use. All AI outputs are suggestions only and must be reviewed by qualified healthcare professionals. AI models may have biases and produce false positives or negatives. The system should never be used as the sole basis for medical decisions.

How HIRA Works

A streamlined workflow that keeps doctors in control while leveraging AI for intelligent assistance.

Upload Report

Patient uploads medical report (X-ray, CT, MRI, or blood test)

AI Analysis

System detects modality and applies appropriate AI model

History Compare

AI compares with patient's previous reports for context

Generate Report

AI creates summary with severity, confidence, and disclaimers

Doctor Review

Doctor reviews AI findings and makes final clinical decision
